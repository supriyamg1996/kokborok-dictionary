KokBorok Dictionary

Kokborok is the official languages of Tripura which is spoken by over 10 million people all over Tripura, Assam, and some parts of Bangladesh. The language is one of the recognised state language & taught in many schools , colleges & university. The Online Kokborok Dictionary Kok Dictionary is the 1st online initiative taken so far to grow the Indigenous language from the state. Stating the Kokborok Online Site is supported by a talkback and translations feature apart from Dictionary. Taking with the creator Jupiter Debbarma an alumnus of Regional Institute of Science & Technology he said ” It will not only boost Kokborok language and culture but also it will make Kokborok ease to learn among the mass audiences.” The online KokBorok Dictionary site is already loved among the Kokborok Lovers & language enthusiasts and grew over 10 k + audiences on Facebook with 50 k Indian Alexa site ranking in a rough 6 months period.
